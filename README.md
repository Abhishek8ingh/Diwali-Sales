The dataset contains sales transactions with the following key attributes:

- **Gender:** Customer gender.
- **Age Group:** Age bracket of the customer.

- **State / Zone:** Geographical location.
- **Occupation:** Customer's profession.
- **Product_Category:** Category of the item sold.
- **Orders:** Number of orders placed.
- **Amount:** Total transaction amount.

## ðŸ“Š Key Insights & Conclusion
Based on the analysis, the following trends were observed:

- **Target Audience:** The majority of buyers are **females**.
- **Age Group:** The most active purchasing age group is **26-35 years**.
- **Location:** High sales volume comes from **Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka**.
- **Profession:** Customers working in **IT, Healthcare, and Aviation** sectors have the highest spending power.
- **Products:** The most popular categories are **Food, Clothing, and Electronics**.

> **Strategic Recommendation:** To maximize sales in the next season, marketing campaigns should target **married women aged 26-35** in **UP, Maharashtra, and Karnataka**, specifically promoting items in the **Food, Clothing, and Electronics** categories.
